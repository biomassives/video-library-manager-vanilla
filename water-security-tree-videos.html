DIY Water Inquiry Outline

  1. A: Start
  2. B: Is the area safe to inspect?
    • Yes ⇒ C: Wear protective gear
      (Then proceed to F: Initial inspection)
    • No ⇒ D: Shut off water supply if needed
      • E: Call for professional help (if urgent)
  3. F: Initial inspection
    (After ensuring safety & protective gear)
  4. G: Check water supply status
    • Some flow ⇒ H: Check main valve
      • J: Is water line clear?
        • Yes ⇒ K: Check flow
          • M: Adequate flow?
            • Yes ⇒ N: Check pressure / next step
              • P: Water supply stable?
                • Yes ⇒ Q: Check for additional leaks
                  • S: Priority level high?
                    • Yes ⇒ T: Urgent fix / expedite repair
                    • No ⇒ U: Plan thorough fix
                      • V: Repair approach
                        • Focused ⇒ W: Targeted repair
                        • Detailed ⇒ X: Full system check
                        Y: Perform final fix ⇒ Z: Test system ⇒ AA: Monitor system ⇒ AB: Restore normal use ⇒ AC: End
                • No ⇒ R: Major fix or replacement
                  (Then the same S: Priority level high? logic follows)
            • No ⇒ O: Fix or replace as needed, then back to N: Check pressure / next step, etc.
        • No ⇒ L: Flush or clear blockage ⇒ (then back to K: Check flow, etc.)
    • No flow ⇒ I: Open main valve ⇒ (loops back to H: Check main valve)
