Fair Use and Copyright:

We believe our use of YouTube videos falls within the principles of fair use, a legal doctrine that allows for the limited use of copyrighted material without permission from the copyright holder for purposes such as education, criticism, and commentary.

We adhere to the following guidelines to ensure our content aligns with fair use principles:

  • Transformative Use: We encourage users to engage with videos in a transformative way, such as adding commentary, analysis, or incorporating them into lesson plans.
  • Educational Context: Videos are presented within a clear educational context, supporting teaching, learning, and research.
  • Attribution: We provide clear attribution to the original YouTube creators, including channel names, video titles, and direct links to the original videos.
  • Limited Use: We use thumbnails to represent subjects and guide users to relevant content, not to replace the full videos.

Disclaimer:

While we believe our practices comply with fair use principles, we cannot guarantee that every use of a YouTube video on our platform will be considered fair use. Users are responsible for their use of copyrighted materials and should comply with YouTube's terms of service.
